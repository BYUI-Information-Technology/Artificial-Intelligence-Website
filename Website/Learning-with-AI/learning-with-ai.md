# Learning with AI

AI tools can support your learning at BYU-Idaho -- helping you study more effectively, explore ideas, and build skills for the workplace. These resources will help you understand the tools available to you, use them responsibly, and make the most of your education.

## Available AI Tools

As a BYU-Idaho student, you have access to two AI tools through your institutional accounts:

- **Google Gemini** -- Sign in with your BYU-Idaho email address. Gemini is a capable AI assistant with strong data protections. Your account also includes **NotebookLM**, which is especially useful for research and study.
- **Microsoft Copilot** -- Available as part of BYU-Idaho's Microsoft 365 suite. Sign in with your institutional account to get started.

Always sign in with your BYU-Idaho account -- personal accounts do not have the same data protections.

For access instructions, see [Access Gemini](/Resources/gemini) and [Access Copilot](/Resources/copilot).
