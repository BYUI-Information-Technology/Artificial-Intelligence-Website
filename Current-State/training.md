Copilot is a Generative AI service provided by Microsoft. Copilot AI services have four distinct versions: Copilot (Personal), Copilot (Enterprise), Copilot for Microsoft 365, and Copilot Studio. Under BYU-Idaho's current Microsoft licensing, the university has access to Copilot Enterprise. Copilot Enterprise provides the same features as the personal edition, but according to BYU-Idaho's contractual agreement, should not store or use data entered into the service to train AI language models.
