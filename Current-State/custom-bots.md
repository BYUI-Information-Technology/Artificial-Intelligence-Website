# Custom Bots

Custom bots offer a powerful way to streamline tasks by automating specific procedures, applying unique knowledge, and even taking on different personalities. They can significantly reduce the time and effort required to complete various processes. However, while custom bots have great potential, they are not yet scalable in their current form. Many developers incur costs for each conversation, and users often need a paid subscription or face limited interactions under free plans. BYU-Idaho does not currently have a scalable solution for creating custom bots.

Below, we have curated a selection of custom bots that showcase their capabilities. Feel free to try them out, keeping in mind that they are provided here for demonstration purposes only.
