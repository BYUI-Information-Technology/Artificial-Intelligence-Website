# How to Access Gemini with Your BYU-Idaho Account

**Audience:** All BYU-Idaho users (employees and students)
**Prerequisites:** An active BYU-Idaho account. Unlike ChatGPT Edu, Gemini does not require a separate license request -- it is included with BYU-Idaho's Google education licenses and available to all students and employees.

---

## Step 1 — Go to Gemini

Open your browser and navigate to **[gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com)**. You'll land on the Gemini homepage.

In the **top-right corner**, click the **"Sign in"** button.

> **Tip:** If you're already signed in with a personal Google account, click your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select **"Add another account"** to add your BYU-Idaho account.

---

## Step 2 — Enter Your BYU-Idaho Email Address

On the Google sign-in page, enter your **BYU-Idaho email address** in this format:

```
email@byui.edu
```

Replace `email` with your BYU-Idaho email address (e.g., `email@byui.edu`). Then click **Next**.

> **Important:** Use your BYU-Idaho **email address**, not your username. Google authentication requires the email format.

---

## Step 3 — Complete BYU-Idaho Authentication

After entering your BYU-Idaho email you may be redirected to the Church login page. Depending on your session state, you may:

- Be signed in automatically if you already have an active BYU-Idaho SSO session in your browser
- Be prompted to enter your Church credentials and complete multi-factor authentication (MFA)

Follow the prompts to enter your Church credentials and complete **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** if prompted.

---

## Step 4 — Confirm You're Using Your Institutional Account

Once authentication is complete, your browser will return to **gemini.google.com**. You'll know the institutional login was successful when:

- The **top-right profile icon** shows your BYU-Idaho account avatar or initials
- Clicking the profile icon displays your `@byui.edu` email address

This confirms you are using the institutional account with BYU-Idaho's data protections.

---

## Returning Users — Switching Accounts

If you use Gemini with both a personal and institutional Google account, make sure the correct account is active before each session. Click the **profile icon** in the top-right corner to see which account is selected, and switch to your `@byui.edu` account if needed.

---

## Important: Use Your Institutional Account

Gemini's approved data classification levels (Public and Internal) only apply when you are signed in with your BYU-Idaho account. If you use a personal Google account, no institutional data protections are in place -- treat it as Public-level only.

---

## Troubleshooting

**I can't sign in with my BYU-Idaho email.**
Verify you are entering your BYU-Idaho email address (not your username). If the issue persists, your Google Workspace account may need to be provisioned -- contact the IT Help Center.

**I'm signed in but not sure if I'm on the institutional account.**
Click the profile icon in the top-right corner. Your `@byui.edu` email address should be displayed. If you see a personal email instead, switch accounts.

**Gemini says my account doesn't have access.**
Contact the [IT Help Center](https://td.byui.edu/SBTDClient/79/ITHelpCenter/Home/?ToUrl=) for assistance.
