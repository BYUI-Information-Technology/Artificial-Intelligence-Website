# How to Access Gemini with Your BYU-Idaho Account

**Audience:** BYU-Idaho employees

**Prerequisites:** An active BYU-Idaho employee account. Unlike ChatGPT Edu, Gemini does not require a separate license request -- it is included with BYU-Idaho's Google education licenses and available to all employees automatically.

---

## Signing In

1. Go to **[gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com)**.
2. Click **"Sign in"** in the top-right corner.
   - If you're already signed in with a personal Google account, click your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select **"Add another account."**
3. Enter your **BYU-Idaho email address** (e.g., `lastname+firstinitial@byui.edu`) and click **Next**.
4. Complete authentication with your BYU-Idaho credentials through the standard sign-in flow.

Once signed in, confirm you see your BYU-Idaho account in the top-right profile icon. This ensures you are using the institutional account with BYU-Idaho's data protections.

---

## Important: Use Your Institutional Account

Gemini's approved data classification levels (Public and Internal) only apply when you are signed in with your BYU-Idaho account. If you use a personal Google account, no institutional data protections are in place -- treat it as Public-level only.
