# AI in Academics: Shaping the Future of Learning at BYU-Idaho

At BYU-Idaho, we are strategically integrating Artificial Intelligence to enhance academic excellence and prepare our students for an evolving world. Our focus is on three core priorities that empower both students and faculty in the age of AI.

## Our Student-Centered, Future-Ready AI Priorities

![head-brain-mind-byui-blue.png](https://brightspotcdn.byui.edu/dims4/default/253e499/2147483647/strip/true/crop/1108x1108+0+0/resize/108x108!/quality/90/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbyu-idaho-brightspot-production-us-east-2.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com%2F81%2F4a%2F8bd9d7f44c889e34cea2806ed620%2Fhead-brain-mind-byui-blue.png)

### Advance Knowledge & Enhance Learning

We are committed to helping every student and faculty member gain essential AI literacy. This involves equipping our community with the foundational knowledge and skills necessary to utilize AI as a learning tool. The goal is to help students become **agents to act**, not simply objects to be acted upon by technology, as they advance in their education.

![lighthouse-advisement-byui-blue.png](https://brightspotcdn.byui.edu/dims4/default/1681282/2147483647/strip/true/crop/1108x1108+0+0/resize/108x108!/quality/90/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbyu-idaho-brightspot-production-us-east-2.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com%2Ff7%2F5f%2F632ed1ca450ea6eb3a237cfc9f60%2Flighthouse-advisement-byui-blue.png)

### Develop Workforce Readiness

The demand for AI-related skills is rapidly increasing across all industries. We are actively strengthening our connections with industry partners to integrate the practical applications of AI within our academic fields. We will use this knowledge to inform our program and course outcomes, assessments, and teaching practices. This ensures our graduates are workplace-ready and prepared to use AI for **righteous work**, bringing their skills and faith to a competitive, ever-changing landscape.

![target-byui-blue.png](https://brightspotcdn.byui.edu/dims4/default/9a6e72b/2147483647/strip/true/crop/1108x1108+0+0/resize/108x108!/quality/90/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbyu-idaho-brightspot-production-us-east-2.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com%2F9f%2F0b%2F5321db0b4eb18c87834db9ca301d%2Ftarget-byui-blue.png)

### Facilitate Student Support & Advising

Our goal is to leverage AI's **untold possibilities** to optimize student support and advising. We are exploring applications such as personalized academic plans, career path guidance, and timely support services. By using AI to enhance meaningful human interaction, we can help students navigate their educational journey and receive the guidance they need to succeed.
