### GenAI at BYU-Idaho

Artificial Intelligence, specifically Generative AI (GenAI), is rapidly evolving and becoming increasingly important in our everyday lives. Find out how you can safely and responsibly use Generative AI at BYU-Idaho.

- [About GenAI](https://www.byui.edu/genai/about): Find training materials, discover what AI is, and access developer information.
- [GenAI Training](https://www.byui.edu/genai/training): This collection of materials will be beneficial in helping those at different levels learn about various topics relating to GenAI.
- [Academic Integrity and AI Use at BYU-Idaho](https://www.byui.edu/genai/academic-integrity): Rules and guidelines for ethical use of Gen AI in the classroom; what to do, and what not to do.
- [GenAI and Copyright](https://www.byui.edu/genai/copyright): Both training and using AI services--especially GenAI-- can put you at risk for copyright, right to privacy, or right of publicity lawsuits.
- [GenAI and Privacy](https://www.byui.edu/genai/privacy): Artificial intelligence relies heavily on data, making it crucial to follow key privacy principles to protect personal information and prevent misuse.
- [GenAI Products](https://www.byui.edu/genai/products): Many AI products exist and many more are in development. Their functionality and purpose vary greatly.
